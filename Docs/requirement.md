AI Resume & Interview Coach
Problem Statement

Many students and job seekers struggle to improve their resumes and prepare effectively for interviews. Existing tools either provide generic feedback or require expensive subscriptions.

The AI Resume & Interview Coach aims to help users improve their resumes, identify skill gaps, practice interviews, receive personalized feedback, and track improvement over time.

Objectives
Analyze resumes
Identify missing skills
Generate interview questions
Evaluate interview responses
Track performance
Improve job readiness
